About

Hello, this is Ed. I have been making software non-stop since 1999, while finishing my CS degree. I count myself very lucky that I can make a living on something I love doing. I have an innate need of building useful stuff and being a productive person.

Rather than focusing on technology X or Y, I seek to learn things that will make me better at reasoning about
problems and solutions. It just so happens that these things are often programming languages that present a new, challenging paradigm that forces me to rewire how I think and approach problems differently.

Here I write about software engineering, architecture, coding and showcase personal projects.

Please a have a look around, you may find something interesting or even useful.
