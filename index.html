About

Hello, this is Ed. I been have making software since 1999, while I was finishing my CS degree at University. I count myself very lucky that I can make a living on something I love doing. Here I write about software engineering, architecture, coding and showcase personal projects.

Rather than focusing on technology X or Y, I seek to learn things that will make me better at reasoning about
problems and solutions. Or help me think better, in general. Languages, programming or otherwise, are a great way to do this since they rewire your brain, provided they present you with a new, challenging paradigm.

Please a have a look around, you may find something interesting or even useful.
